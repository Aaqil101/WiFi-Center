Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, try the following steps:

  • Ensure your WiFi is turned on.
  • Restart the application.
  • Check your network settings.
  • Consult the FAQ section for common issues.

FAQ

Q: How do I exit?

A: Click the 'X' button or press Alt+F4.

Q: Where is the settings menu?

A: Click on the top-right menu icon.

Q: How do I scan for networks?

A: Go to the Network Scanner feature and click 'Scan'.

Q: How do I connect to a network?

A: Select a network from the Network Scanner results and click 'Connect'.
